The founder

Abir Moussaoui is a student deeply passionate about intellectual debates and behavioral sciences. With years of rigorous studies and active involvement in social causes, she has developed a strong interest in addressing contemporary social and human challenges. Abir is recognized for her analytical mindset and her desire to engage with complex questions, always seeking meaningful solutions. Her project, "Fracas d'idées," embodies this commitment to collective and constructive thinking.
